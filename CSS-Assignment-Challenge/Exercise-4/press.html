Stellar Labs Announces Breakthrough in Quantum Computing

San Francisco, CA – June 15, 2025

Stellar Labs today announced a major advancement in quantum error correction that could accelerate practical quantum computing by a decade.

Key Highlights

The new technique reduces qubit error rates by 94% in laboratory conditions, bringing fault-tolerant quantum computing closer to reality than ever before.

What This Means

Industries from pharmaceuticals to cryptography will feel the impact. The company plans to open its platform to select partners by early 2026.
